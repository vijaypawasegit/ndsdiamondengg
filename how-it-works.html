How It Works

Choose Ingredients

Start by selecting your favorite tea leaves, herbs, and other ingredients to create your perfect blend. You can use our pre-made recipes or customize your own unique blend to suit your taste.

Add Ingredients to the Machine

Once you've selected your ingredients, simply add them to the tea brewing chamber of the machine. Be sure to follow the recommended ratios and brewing instructions for the best results.

Start the Machine

Once your preferences are set, press the start button and watch as the machine blends your ingredients together into a perfectly brewed cup of tea. The machine will beep when the brewing process is complete.

Enjoy Your Perfect Cup of Tea

Once the brewing process is complete, simply pour your freshly brewed tea into your favorite cup and enjoy! The machine's large capacity allows you to brew multiple cups at once, so you can share your delicious blends with friends and family.
